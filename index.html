Why Us

Find your Place

You take your education seriously, and so do we.

At Purdue University Fort Wayne*, we combine the international prestige and challenging academics of Purdue University with personalized attention and hands-on opportunities.

Located in up-and-coming Fort Wayne, Indiana, we’re big-city in culture and lifestyle, but small-city in affordability, connectivity, and support. 

When you leave Purdue Fort Wayne, you'll have a degree from one of the most respected names in education—opening doors to countless opportunities and experiences. 

Every journey is important. We’re prepared for yours.

Student Life

See yourself Here

Student organizations. Events. Athletics. Your own private room.

Many of our students have their own private bedroom (and bathroom) in our on-campus, apartment-style living spaces. Walk or bike to class. Catch the bus to Fort Wayne for a burger at Powers or a banh mi at Kim Vu.

Join in on an intramural sport or take advantage of free admission to all of the Mastodons' home games—whatever sport is your thing. And we've got 14 other men's and women's sports teams to cheer on all year long.

We know student life is just as important as your academic one.

Fort Wayne

Explore the City

Small-city vibes. Big-city opportunities.

Fort Wayne is the second-largest city in Indiana, and there's so much to see and do here. We believe in strong community connections, which is why we're so proud of our 250+ partnerships with regional employers and industries.

You'll intern, network, and receive some of your most formative experiences and opportunities in this city. And you'll definitely catch a TinCaps baseball game or Mad Ants basketball game—it's Purdue Fort Wayne tradition.

We know you're going to love this city as much as we do. Here's a few of the items on our Fort Wayne must-do list.

Your Backyard

Through new Eyes

We can talk all day about why we love Fort Wayne.

But you're from here. You know already that this city is on an upswing: businesses are flourishing. Arts and culture have taken hold. New opportunities are popping up all over the place. It's a nice place to call home.

At Purdue University Fort Wayune*, you're gettting all the prestige and pedigree of Purdue's big name, but with convenience, cozy class sizes, and affordability.

Learn from some of the best faculty in the country; intern with businesses you've admired your whole life; make new friends from your hometown and beyond. There's nothing you can't do here.
