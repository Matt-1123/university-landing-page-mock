Why Us

Find your Place

You take your education seriously, and so do we.

At Purdue University Fort Wayne*, we combine the international prestige and challenging academics of Purdue University with personalized attention and hands-on opportunities.

Located in up-and-coming Fort Wayne, Indiana, we’re big-city in culture and lifestyle, but small-city in affordability, connectivity, and support. 

When you leave Purdue Fort Wayne, you'll have a degree from one of the most respected names in education—opening doors to countless opportunities and experiences. 

Every journey is important. We’re prepared for yours.
